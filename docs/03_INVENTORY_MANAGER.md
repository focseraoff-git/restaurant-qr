The **Inventory Manager** helps you track stock, manage suppliers, and prevent shortages using smart alerts.

## 1. Inventory Dashboard
The main screen gives you a high-level overview of your stock health.
*   **Total Items**: Count of all registered SKUs.
*   **Total Value**: Estimated value of stock on hand (based on latest purchase price).
*   **Low Stock Alerts**: A prioritized list of items running low. Items with specific "Action" buttons (e.g., `Order Stock`) appear here.

## 2. Managing Items (`Item Master`)
Go to the **"Items"** tab to manage your product catalog.
1.  **Add New Item**:
    *   Click **+ Add Item**.
    *   Enter **Name** (e.g., "Basmati Rice").
    *   **Unit**: kg, liters, pcs, etc.
    *   **Min Stock Level**: The threshold at which the system should alert you.
    *   **Current Stock**: Initial quantity.
2.  **Edit/Delete**: Click the pencil or trash icon on any item row.

## 3. Stock Movements (In/Out)
Track every gram of ingredient usage.
*   **Stock In**: When a delivery arrives.
    *   Select Item -> Enter Quantity -> Enter Supplier & Cost.
    *   This increases the `Current Stock` and updates `Total Value`.
*   **Stock Out**: For wastage, improved usage tracking, or manual corrections.
    *   Select Item -> Enter Quantity -> Reason (e.g., "Spoilage", "Recipe Use").

## 4. Suppliers
Maintain a digital directory of your vendors.
*   **Add Supplier**: Name, Phone, Category (e.g., "Vegetable Vendor").
*   **Link Items**: (Advanced) Associate specific items with suppliers for one-click reordering.

## 5. Tips for Success
*   **Set Realistic Min Levels**: Ensure your "Low Stock" threshold gives you enough lead time to reorder before running out.
*   **Regular Audits**: Use the "Stock Out" feature to adjust for spoilage or theft weekly to keep numbers accurate.
