The **Billing & Controls** module is the financial headquarters of your restaurant. It combines sales analytics, expense tracking, and credit management (Khata).

## 1. Sales Analytics Dashboard
Visualize your restaurant's performance.
*   **Revenue Cards**: Total Revenue, Total Orders, Average Order Value.
*   **Time Filters**: Toggle between `Daily`, `Monthly`, and `Yearly` views.
*   **Graphs**: (Visual) Sales trends over time to identify peak hours/days.

## 2. Recent Bills & Transactions
*   **Bill Search**: Find any past bill by ID or Date.
*   **Audit**: See cancelled bills and the reason for cancellation (prevents fraud).

## 3. Khata Manager (Credit Ledger)
Manage loyal customers who run a tab.
*   **Customer Directory**: List of all customers with active credit accounts.
    *   **Green Badge**: Active Credit Line.
    *   **Current Due**: Total amount they owe you.
*   **Add Transaction**:
    *   **Credit (+)**: Add a new meal cost to their tab.
    *   **Payment (-)**: Record a payment they made (Cash/UPI) to reduce their balance.
*   **Limit**: Set a credit limit (e.g., â‚¹5000) to prevent over-lending.

## 4. Expenses (Petty Cash)
Track outflows to get a true Net Profit.
*   **Log Expense**: Enter Amount, Category (e.g., "Groceries", "Repair"), and Description.
*   **Reports**: See how much you are spending vs earning.

## 5. Security
*   **Secure Mode**: Accessing this module may require a PIN (if enabled) to protect sensitive financial data.
