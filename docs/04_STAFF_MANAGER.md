View and manage your team profiles.

## 4. Roles & Permissions
*   **Admins**: Full access to all modules.
*   **Managers**: Can access Billing, Staff, and Inventory.
*   **Waiters/Chefs**: Restricted access (if login is enabled) to Counter and Kitchen views respectively.
